Old Tucson

Enjoy rich history, green cacti, and old movies; an all you can learn buffet at Old Tucson Studios!

Four exciting activites to do whilst visiting Old Tucson

Old Tucson Studio Tours

Tour the sets and scenery of movies and TV shows such as: Tombstone, Little House on the Prairie, and Three Amigos.

Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum

Take a long shaded walk through nature at the Desert Museam nearby Old Tucson, full of desert animals of all kinds.

Yultide Christmas at Old Tucson

Come during the winter months for a Holiday themed blast to the past.

Gate Pass Trailhead

Hike along one of the most beautiful paths for sunrises and sunsets on the Gate Pass Trailhead.
